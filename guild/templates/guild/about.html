About MUST Students Guild

Empowering students at Mbarara University of Science and Technology to lead, innovate, and make a lasting impact on campus and beyond.

Mbarara University of Science and Technology

Our Legacy

Established in 1989, Mbarara University of Science and Technology (MUST) is one of Uganda's premier public universities. Located in the beautiful southwestern town of Mbarara, the university has grown from humble beginnings to become a center of excellence in science, technology, innovation, and health sciences education in East Africa.

The university was founded with a special mandate to address the regional and national shortages in medical personnel and advance technological innovations tailored to solving local challenges.

Academic Excellence

MUST is renowned for its world-class education across six faculties:

  • Faculty of Medicine
  • Faculty of Science
  • Faculty of Computing and Informatics
  • Faculty of Applied Sciences and Technology
  • Faculty of Business and Management Sciences
  • Faculty of Interdisciplinary Studies

With over 4,000 students and distinguished faculty members, MUST continues to produce skilled graduates who contribute significantly to Uganda's development agenda.

Community Impact

As part of its community service mission, MUST operates the Mbarara Regional Referral Hospital, providing essential healthcare services to the southwestern region while training medical students in real-world settings.

The university prioritizes research and innovation in addressing local challenges, with numerous community outreach programs that directly impact surrounding communities through health initiatives, technological solutions, and environmental conservation projects.

The MUST Students Guild

The MUST Students Guild serves as the official representative body for all students at Mbarara University of Science and Technology. Founded alongside the university in 1989, the Guild has evolved into a powerful platform that advocates for student welfare, promotes academic excellence, and fosters leadership development.

Our vision is to create a vibrant student community where every voice matters and where students actively participate in shaping the future of their education and society. The Guild operates on principles of inclusivity, accountability, and innovation.

We organize various academic, cultural, sports, and social events throughout the academic year, including the annual MUST Cultural Gala, Science and Technology Exhibition, and Inter-Faculty Sports Tournament. These events provide opportunities for students to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and build lasting connections.

The Guild also plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between students and university administration, ensuring that student concerns are addressed promptly and effectively. Through regular consultations and feedback mechanisms, we work collaboratively with university leadership to improve the overall student experience at MUST.

Guild Leadership Structure

The MUST Students Guild operates through a democratically elected leadership team that represents the diverse student body. Elections are held annually, allowing students to choose leaders who will champion their interests and concerns.
